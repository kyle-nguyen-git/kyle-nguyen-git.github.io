1 Item 2 Guys

A pop-up food concept built around simple menus, tight execution, and high standards.

What it is

1 Item 2 Guys is a pop-up concept we run with one goal: deliver a restaurant-quality experience without a bloated menu. The constraint is the point — it forces prep, timing, and consistency.

We test recipes, run small batches, and iterate quickly based on what people actually buy and what we can execute at a high level during service.

  • Streamlined menu → faster lines and better consistency
  • Execution-first mindset → prep systems, station roles, and checklists
  • Iterative approach → improve each pop-up based on feedback + throughput

The “One Item” philosophy

One-item menus aren’t a gimmick — they’re operational leverage. With fewer variables, we can tighten the entire workflow: prep lists, ingredient sourcing, station roles, timing, and quality control. That’s how you go from “good” to “repeatable.”

How we run a pop-up

Systems

  • Prep lists + pars for every ingredient
  • Station roles and handoffs (line flow matters)
  • Timing targets (throughput without quality drop)
  • Post-pop-up debrief: what slowed us down, what sold, what ran out

What we’re optimizing

  • Consistency across every plate
  • Shorter ticket times + cleaner service rhythm
  • Ingredient efficiency (less waste)
  • Better customer experience (simple ordering, fast pickup)
