1 Item 2 Guys

A small pop-up project that turned into one of my favorite ways to create something fun with friends and meet people through food.

How it started

The idea started after going to a ramen pop-up on campus — it was simple, packed, and felt like an actual experience instead of just grabbing food. After spending a summer in New York and seeing how common pop-ups are there, I wanted to try building something like that back in Austin.

Since then, this has turned into a chance for us to create a unique night, connect with people, and grow with the community around us.

If you want to see the food and vibe, the Instagram is basically the “home base.”
