Hope Full Farms

Supporting outreach and operations for a community-focused nonprofit program, with an emphasis on partnerships, school engagement, and sponsor outreach for the Junior Chef Challenge.

What I Worked On

My work centered on building a repeatable outreach pipeline and helping the team stay organized as we expanded to new schools and community partners.

  • Built and maintained outreach lists (schools, staff contacts, community organizations).
  • Wrote and iterated email templates for first touch + follow-ups.
  • Planned follow-up call workflows and helped coordinate timing around school calendars.
  • Researched sponsor prospects and supported early sponsorship outreach.
  • Kept internal tracking clean so we could see what was sent, what needs a call, and what’s next.

Why It Matters

For programs like this, execution is everything. A good message doesn’t help if it doesn’t land in the right inbox, at the right time, with a clear next step. The goal was to reduce friction for schools and partners while keeping our team’s outreach consistent and easy to manage.

Tools and Approach

  • Simple tracking systems (spreadsheets, notes, lightweight templates).
  • Short, clear outreach messaging with an obvious call-to-action.
  • Follow-up cadence that’s respectful of school schedules.
  • Organizing contacts so calls/emails can be handed off without losing context.

Next Steps

As we keep expanding, the focus is on improving response rates and making outreach more scalable without losing the personal touch — tighter lists, cleaner messaging, and faster follow-through.
