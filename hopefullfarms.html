Hope Full Farm

Through Project Advance Austin at UT Austin, I serve as a project management consultant supporting Hope Full Farm’s Farm to Fork: Junior Chef Challenge.

My role is a mix of building systems and executing: I set up organizing documents and spreadsheets to track corporate outreach, influencer outreach, and junior chef applications—then I help run the outreach itself through cold calls and email follow-ups.

About Hope Full Farm

Hope Full Farm is a Central Texas nonprofit focused on addressing childhood hunger and growing community impact through its farm-based work and programs.

  • Mission: fight childhood hunger and support future regenerative farmers.
  • Visit Hope Full Farm
  • Farm to Fork Event

Farm to Fork: Junior Chef Challenge

The Farm to Fork Junior Chef Challenge is a fundraising event that pairs youth competitors with local chefs to create dishes using Hope Full Farm’s organic produce. It’s designed to be family-friendly while directly supporting the farm’s mission and programs.
